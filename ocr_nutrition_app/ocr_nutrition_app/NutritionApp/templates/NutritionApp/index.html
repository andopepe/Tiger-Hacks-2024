Our goal is to help people make better food choices by providing easy access to nutritional information. Just scan a barcode to quickly learn what's in your food and make smarter decisions for a healthier life.

What is a UPC Code?

A UPC (Universal Product Code) is a barcode found on most retail products. It consists of 12 digits, which uniquely identify the item and its manufacturer.

Where to Find the UPC Code

The UPC is usually located near the bottom of the packaging, often on the back or sides. It is a black-and-white barcode with numbers beneath it.
